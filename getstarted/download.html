Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have successfully created a Boxfuse Account (simply log in with your GitHub account, it's free).

Downloading the Boxfuse Client

There are two ways to download the latest Boxfuse Client.

Personalized download (recommended)

The easiest way to download the Boxfuse Client is to download your personalized copy from the Boxfuse Console.

Download

It already contains your Boxfuse Account credentials and is ready to be used.

Unpersonlized downloads

Alternatively you can also download an unpersonalized copy:

boxfuse-commandline-{{site.boxfuseVersion}}.zip (no JRE)
boxfuse-commandline-{{site.boxfuseVersion}}.tar.gz (no JRE)
boxfuse-commandline-{{site.boxfuseVersion}}-windows-x64.zip
boxfuse-commandline-{{site.boxfuseVersion}}-macosx-x64.tar.gz
boxfuse-commandline-{{site.boxfuseVersion}}-linux-x64.tar.gz

These unpersonalized downloads require you to enter your credentials to work with your Boxfuse account:
# install-dir/conf/boxfuse.conf
user=your-boxfuse-user
secret=your-boxfuse-secret

You can find your credentials on the downloads tab in the Boxfuse Console.

Installing the Boxfuse Client

Once you have downloaded the Boxfuse Client simply:

  1. Extract the download to a directory you have write permissions in
  2. Add the extracted directory to the PATH
  3. Verify the installation by executing the boxfuse -v in a new terminal/command prompt window.

And that's it! Boxfuse has now been successfully installed and is ready to be used.
